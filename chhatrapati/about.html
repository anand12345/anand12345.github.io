About US

Vision:

ChhatrapatiShivaji Science College, Latur will be widely recognized as a top class science college in the Latur city and in Maharashtra state with a model curriculum for the interweaving of liberal education and fundamental knowledge with practical educationwith focus on overall student development.

Mission:

The mission of ChhatrapatiShivaji Science College is to be a socially responsible, creative disseminator of knowledge and to equip our students with tools to take up challenges of the enormous academia and to empower them to excel and contribute to future technological, social and academic progress.

Core values:

  • Being a vibrant knowledge cluster and an excellent education provider.
  • Spreading knowledge and encouragement to students.
  • Providing high quality and value based education.
  • Being curious.
  • Support open intellectual inquiry and expression of individual student.
  • Sustain excellence in teaching, research, and public engagement.
  • Enlighten students with knowledge.
  • Reward curiosity, merit, creativity, and innovation.
  • Implementing a collaborative attitude in students.
  • Striving for high academic standards.
